Zooet The Cab

What We Do

Zooet the Cab is a Khayelitsha based company, whose mission is to provide reliable, timely, and safe cab services in our townships. The cab is available to all and transports people anywhere in Khayelitsha. Operational hours are 09:00 pm – 04:00 am. Unlike traditional cabs, you pay a fixed amount per person, per trip.

As a transport company, our philosophy is one that emphasizes service, and a realization that effective communication is a key component in our business. Our cab is always a phone call away.

Zooet has drivers and employees that are helpful, courteous, and fully trained on customer care and driving. All our cabs are clean, well maintained and inspected regularly for safety and comfort.
